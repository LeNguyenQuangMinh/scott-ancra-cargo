2-Inch x 30-Foot Chain Anchor Ratchet Strap

With grab hooks fixed on the ends of the chain, your tie down jobs are even more secure on both sides of your load with the Kinedyne 2-inch x 30-foot Chain Anchor Ratchet Strap with a working load limit of 3,335 Lbs. / 1,513 Kgs.

Our easily identifiable Kinedyne Gold with Black Edge 2084F Webbing lets you know that this Chain Anchor Ratchet Strap is both strong and reliable with a standard 18” fixed end. Golden dichromate zinc plated heat-treated carbon steel Kinedyne 3705-2 Chain Anchors with 5/16 inch Grade 43 chain hooks are all the hardware our 11.1 pound / 5.0 kilogram 2” x 30’ Chain Anchor Ratchet Strap needs in order to withstand some of nature’s strongest challenges to ensure that your load remains secure.

Kinedyne tightly weaves its own webbing and designs the sew patterns for our 2” X 30’ Chain Anchor Ratchet Strap webbing. We assume direct quality control over the manufacturing in order to certify that the ratchet straps exceed the demands of many tie down applications.

The Kinedyne 533039 has a tested working load limit of 3,335 Lbs. / 1,513 Kgs. Our Ratchet Straps are stenciled and tagged with the Kinedyne name and logo as well as the Working Load Limit to meet DOT regulations and CVSA, CHP, North American Cargo Securement, and WSTDA standards.

2” by 30’ Chain Anchor Ratchet Strap – 533039 Quick Specs:

Working Load Limit: 3,335 Lbs. / 1,513 Kgs

Width: 2 Inches

Length: 30 Feet

Weight: 11.1 Lbs. / 5.0 Kgs.

Webbing: Standard Kinedyne Gold with Black Edge 2084F Webbing.

Fixed End Length: 18 Inches.

Type of Tension Device: Kinedyne 802 Standard Handle Ratchet

End Fitting: Kinedyne 3705-2 Chain Anchor
